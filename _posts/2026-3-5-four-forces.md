In physics, they say, without four fundamental forces holding together the universe, matter would fly apart, spin out - there would be no Big Bang, no atomic particles, no galaxies and stars and planetary orbits, no gases nor solids nor liquids, and ultimately, no life. 

These four fundamental forces, likely unified right at the Big Bang before separating out, govern all interactions between matter in the universe. They are: gravity, electromagnetism, strong nuclear force, and weak nuclear force.

![NASA Centaurus A](/images/cena-lic-lp-nature-cropped.png)

I believe society is governed by four analogous forces. Without these forces, society, community would not form -- we would all be individuals dispersed to the wind, not connecting; not forming teams, coalitions; lacking civilization, achievement. These are the forces that keep us together, than run our world, that determine the arc of our actions, the flow of our resources. 

These forces are:

1. Love (gravity). Infinite range -- even if you are far apart, you can feel the pull of this force. This accounts for family ties, friends, culture, movements. While ostensibly the weakest of the four, at its strongest it can overcome any of the other three forces. It arguably drives some of the largest impacts on the world. 

2. Proximity (weak force). This is the association arising from close physical or digital distance. It is short-range, and weak, but like the proverbial butterfly in Chaos theory, has myriad effects on people's lives. As long as people are close enough to run into each other, unplanned, this force is in play, but quickly dissipates with distance if there is not another force in play.

3. Belief (electromagnetism). This is one's thoughts and beliefs. Like electromagnetism, this can both draw and repel people -- causing battles, wars, and conflicts. This has infinite range, and is quite strong.

4. Necessity (strong force). This is the drive to survive. It is very strong, and short range. This includes what is needed to live: food, shelter, resources, a mate, etc. In our modern world it can translate to safe surroundings and a job/career. This also includes short-range defensive and offensive actions.

Together, these are the forces which allow people to gain the density and mobility to become something greater than the sum of their parts.
